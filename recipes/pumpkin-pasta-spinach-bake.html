Pumpkin, pasta and spinach bake

Description

In a Pumpkin, Pasta, and Spinach Bake, you'll find a cozy blend of flavors that come together effortlessly. It starts with pasta, cooked just right to provide a comforting base. Then, there's the roasted pumpkin, bringing in a touch of sweetness and a creamy texture that melds beautifully with the pasta's heartiness.

Adding to the mix is the tender spinach, adding color and a fresh, earthy taste. Once baked until golden, this dish becomes a simple yet satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion. With every forkful, you'll taste the warmth of autumn and the joy of comfort food done just right.

Ingredients

  • 250 g frozen spinach drained thawed
  • 1/4 cup dried breadcrumbs
  • 30 g margarine
  • 250 g pumpkin chopped peeled
  • 3 garlic cloves crushed
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 240 g fresh ricotta
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup CARNATION Light & Creamy Evaporated Milk
  • 1 1/2 cups pasta cooked

Steps

  1. Spray a 30 cm x 20 cm rectangular oven proof dish with non-stick spray and sprinkle breadcrumbs over base.
  2. Melt margarine in a fry pan and add garlic and pumpkin, stir over heat for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Add spinach and stir another 3 minutes or until pumpkin is just tender.
  4. Add nutmeg.
  5. Beat ricotta, milk, and eggs in a large bowl until smooth.
  6. Stir in pasta and the pumpkin mixture.
  7. Spoon into prepared dish and bake about 30 minutes at 180C, until set and lightly browned.
