Curried pumpkin soup

Description

Curried pumpkin soup is a cozy bowl of warmth and flavor, perfect for chilly days or evenings by the fireplace. It starts with creamy pumpkin puree, infused with aromatic curry spices that dance on your taste buds with each spoonful. The curry adds a gentle kick of heat, balanced by the natural sweetness of the pumpkin, creating a harmonious blend of savory and spicy notes.

As the soup simmers on the stove, the fragrant spices fill the air, enticing you with their tantalizing aroma. Garnished with a dollop of creamy yogurt or a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, each bowl becomes a comforting delight for the senses. Whether enjoyed as a starter or a main course, curried pumpkin soup is sure to warm your soul and leave you craving just one more spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 kg jap pumpkin chopped peeled
  • 2 onions chopped
  • 1 tbs garlic crushed
  • 3 tbs curry powder
  • 1L Massel Chicken Style Liquid Stock
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 600 ml cream
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Saute onion, garlic and curry powder in some olive oil until tender.
  2. Add pumpkin, stock, salt and pepper, and bring to the boil, then reduce to a simmer. The liquid will reduce slightly.
  3. When pumpkin has softened, blend the soup.
  4. Stir through cream and simmer.
