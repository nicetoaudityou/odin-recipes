Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is like a delicious Italian puzzle, where each layer adds something special. Picture sheets of pasta cooked just right, nestled between savory sauces and creamy goodness. First, there's the tangy tomato sauce, full of garlic and herbs, creating a flavorful base. Then comes the creamy béchamel sauce, smooth and rich, adding a luxurious touch.

Between these saucy layers, you'll find tasty fillings like seasoned meats or colorful veggies. And let's not forget the cheese—loads of it! Sprinkled between each layer and on top, it melts into a golden crust, making every bite a delight. Lasagna is like a cozy hug on a plate, perfect for sharing with loved ones or savoring all to yourself.

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 garlic clove crushed
  • 125 g mushrooms sliced
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs *to taste
  • 850 g canned chopped tomato
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 sheets lasagne sheets

Steps

  1. Fry the chopped onion and crushed garlic in a little oil for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced beef, mushrooms and herbs.
  3. When meat is brown add 850g chopped tomatoes, sugar, tomato paste and salt to taste, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Cheese sauce: Melt some butter in a pan and stir in flour, milk, grated cheese and a pinch of nutmeg. Stir mixture constantly until thick.
  5. Pour half the meat sauce into a baking dish, then add alternate layers of dry lasagna, meat mixture and cheese sauce, finishing with the cheese sauce.
  6. Sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese and bake at 200C for 35 minutes.

Pumpkin, pasta and spinach bake

Description

In a Pumpkin, Pasta, and Spinach Bake, you'll find a cozy blend of flavors that come together effortlessly. It starts with pasta, cooked just right to provide a comforting base. Then, there's the roasted pumpkin, bringing in a touch of sweetness and a creamy texture that melds beautifully with the pasta's heartiness.

Adding to the mix is the tender spinach, adding color and a fresh, earthy taste. Once baked until golden, this dish becomes a simple yet satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion. With every forkful, you'll taste the warmth of autumn and the joy of comfort food done just right.

Ingredients

  • 250 g frozen spinach drained thawed
  • 1/4 cup dried breadcrumbs
  • 30 g margarine
  • 250 g pumpkin chopped peeled
  • 3 garlic cloves crushed
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 240 g fresh ricotta
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup CARNATION Light & Creamy Evaporated Milk
  • 1 1/2 cups pasta cooked

Steps

  1. Spray a 30 cm x 20 cm rectangular oven proof dish with non-stick spray and sprinkle breadcrumbs over base.
  2. Melt margarine in a fry pan and add garlic and pumpkin, stir over heat for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Add spinach and stir another 3 minutes or until pumpkin is just tender.
  4. Add nutmeg.
  5. Beat ricotta, milk, and eggs in a large bowl until smooth.
  6. Stir in pasta and the pumpkin mixture.
  7. Spoon into prepared dish and bake about 30 minutes at 180C, until set and lightly browned.

Curried pumpkin soup

Description

Curried pumpkin soup is a cozy bowl of warmth and flavor, perfect for chilly days or evenings by the fireplace. It starts with creamy pumpkin puree, infused with aromatic curry spices that dance on your taste buds with each spoonful. The curry adds a gentle kick of heat, balanced by the natural sweetness of the pumpkin, creating a harmonious blend of savory and spicy notes.

As the soup simmers on the stove, the fragrant spices fill the air, enticing you with their tantalizing aroma. Garnished with a dollop of creamy yogurt or a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, each bowl becomes a comforting delight for the senses. Whether enjoyed as a starter or a main course, curried pumpkin soup is sure to warm your soul and leave you craving just one more spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 kg jap pumpkin chopped peeled
  • 2 onions chopped
  • 1 tbs garlic crushed
  • 3 tbs curry powder
  • 1L Massel Chicken Style Liquid Stock
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 600 ml cream
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Saute onion, garlic and curry powder in some olive oil until tender.
  2. Add pumpkin, stock, salt and pepper, and bring to the boil, then reduce to a simmer. The liquid will reduce slightly.
  3. When pumpkin has softened, blend the soup.
  4. Stir through cream and simmer.
