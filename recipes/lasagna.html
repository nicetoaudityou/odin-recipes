Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is like a delicious Italian puzzle, where each layer adds something special. Picture sheets of pasta cooked just right, nestled between savory sauces and creamy goodness. First, there's the tangy tomato sauce, full of garlic and herbs, creating a flavorful base. Then comes the creamy béchamel sauce, smooth and rich, adding a luxurious touch.

Between these saucy layers, you'll find tasty fillings like seasoned meats or colorful veggies. And let's not forget the cheese—loads of it! Sprinkled between each layer and on top, it melts into a golden crust, making every bite a delight. Lasagna is like a cozy hug on a plate, perfect for sharing with loved ones or savoring all to yourself.

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 garlic clove crushed
  • 125 g mushrooms sliced
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs *to taste
  • 850 g canned chopped tomato
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 sheets lasagne sheets

Steps

  1. Fry the chopped onion and crushed garlic in a little oil for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced beef, mushrooms and herbs.
  3. When meat is brown add 850g chopped tomatoes, sugar, tomato paste and salt to taste, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Cheese sauce: Melt some butter in a pan and stir in flour, milk, grated cheese and a pinch of nutmeg. Stir mixture constantly until thick.
  5. Pour half the meat sauce into a baking dish, then add alternate layers of dry lasagna, meat mixture and cheese sauce, finishing with the cheese sauce.
  6. Sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese and bake at 200C for 35 minutes.
